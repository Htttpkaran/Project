About Renewable Energy

Renewable energy is energy from sources that are naturally replenishing but flow-limited. They are virtually inexhaustible in duration but limited in the amount of energy that is available per unit of time.

Types of Renewable Energy

Solar Energy

Solar energy is the most abundant energy resource on earth.

Wind Energy

Wind energy is one of the fastest-growing energy sources in the world.

Hydropower

Hydropower is one of the oldest and largest sources of renewable energy.
